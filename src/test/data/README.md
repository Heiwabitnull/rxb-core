- Network ports: 8327 (P2P), 8328 (RPC)
- Accessible mining - designed for ordinary computers
- Mathematical rules - unchanged by external pressure

What You Can Do
---------------
- Run a node - strengthen the independent network
- Mine coins - build your economic resilience  
- Hold your keys - do not reveal your private keys to anyone
- Use RXB coins for transactions - purchase goods and services
- No permission required - no restrictions can prevent you from using RXB
- Trade fairly - sell or buy RXB coins using PayPal or other services, always acting honestly
- Share the technology - help others gain independence

The mathematical foundation of RXB ensures it remains free from
the influence of those who seek to dominate others. No politician,
billionaire, or corporation can alter its fundamental rules.

You have installed RXB. You now possess the tools to maintain
your financial autonomy regardless of what centralised systems may do.
